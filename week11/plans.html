introducing: my closet.

Idea

  • Need help tracking your closet?
  • Organize your wardrobe efficiently and plan your outfits.
  • See what is left in your wardrobe at any time, even when you're not on laundry day!
  • Upload your clothes and make outfits!

Why?

  • I've been wanting something like this for a while.
  • I have a lot of clothes so I would like to see them organized throughout the week.

How?

  • Through HTML, CSS and JS.
  • Similar to the slide before, I want to use a sliding horizontal shelf to users to scroll through different clothing options.
  • Using JS, I want to experiment with "making" and "saving" an outfit if user chooses to select the outfit.
